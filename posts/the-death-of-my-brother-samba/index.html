DEDICATION

On the 6th of March at 2 pm, I had completed all necessary preparations for my departure to Istanbul. After bidding farewell to a select group of close friends, I embarked on my journey. While at the university, I enjoyed a leisurely lunch and snapped some photographs with a dear friend, Aziz, which I will share with you shortly. After lunch, I returned to my dormitory, where I gathered my luggage and backpacks before making my way to the bus station. Along the way, I ran into another friend, Ali, who insisted on helping me with my heaviest luggage. Despite my initial refusal, Ali’s insistence prevailed, and he kindly carried the luggage for me. To sum up, I arrived at the bus station around 4 pm, boarded the bus, and commenced my journey to Istanbul.

Upon arriving in Istanbul, I stayed at a friend’s flat. The following day, March 7th, I rose early and visited the Beylikdüzü Bazaar for a bit of sightseeing. I returned to the flat around 12 pm, collected my luggage, and headed to the Istanbul airport. Once there, I met up with another friend from Mauritania, Zehraa, and we spent a few minutes together before I checked in and proceeded through security. After completing security procedures, I made my way to the gate to await the start of boarding.

Around 3 pm, the flight to NKTT (presumably Nouakchott, Mauritania) took off, and we landed around 7 pm. I had arranged for my cousin to meet me at the airport, but he was not there upon my arrival. When I called him, he gave me some unfortunate news: he and my family were at the hospital because my brother had been in a serious car accident.
